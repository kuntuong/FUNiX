World Cups

World cups are cups that are held by FIFA. There will be 32 teams in the world cup. Before each world cup, there will be a qualifiers tournament held in each continent, seperately. Then, the top 4 or 3 of those continents will qualify for the world cup. World cups are held every 4 years. The host of the world cups do not need to be in the qualifers round because they are automatically in the World Cup. The country that has won the most world cups is Brazil with 5 world cup wins.

Leagues

The major leagues are in Europe. Those leagues are Ligue 1 (France), Bundesliga (Germany), Premier Leagure (England), La Liga (Spain) and Serie A (Italy). These leagues are not played as normal tournaments. Each team will play eachother.

Champions League and Europa League

Before the Champions League, they also have to do a qualifiers round, the teams that qualify will go to Champions League. The teams that don't will have a chance to be in Europa League. Champions League and Europa League are from clubs, not countries in Europe. This also occurs in other continents but just different names.
